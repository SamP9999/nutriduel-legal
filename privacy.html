Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

Welcome to NutriDuel ("we," "our," or "us"). NutriDuel is a mobile application that helps you track your nutrition, set health goals, and compete with friends in nutrition challenges. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address - Used for account creation and authentication
  • Username - Your chosen display name (visible to friends)
  • Password - Encrypted and stored securely (we cannot see your password)

Health and Nutrition Data

  • Profile information - Height, weight, age, sex, activity level
  • Nutrition goals - Daily calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat targets
  • Meal logs - Foods you track, quantities, and nutritional information
  • Duel data - Your participation in nutrition challenges with friends

Device Information

  • Push notification tokens - To send you notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Device type and operating system - For app functionality and support

Usage Data

  • Analytics data - App usage patterns, feature interactions (collected via PostHog)
  • Error logs - Crash reports and technical issues (collected via Sentry)

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide app functionality - Enable nutrition tracking, goal setting, and social features
  • Send notifications - Friend requests, duel invitations, and daily reminders (with your permission)
  • Improve app experience - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix bugs
  • Support and communication - Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Security - Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

Data Storage

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Supabase - Your data is stored securely in Supabase's encrypted databases
  • Data encryption - All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access controls - Only you can access your personal data through your account

Data Retention

  • Active accounts - We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts - When you delete your account, we permanently delete your personal data within 30 days
  • Backup data - Deleted data may remain in backups for up to 90 days before permanent deletion

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings screen in the app. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect or process your information:

Supabase

  • Purpose - Database, authentication, and backend infrastructure
  • Data shared - All app data (account info, meal logs, goals)
  • Privacy policy - https://supabase.com/privacy

PostHog (Analytics)

  • Purpose - App usage analytics and product insights
  • Data shared - Anonymized usage events (no personal identifiers)
  • Privacy policy - https://posthog.com/privacy
  • Opt-out - You can opt out of analytics in app settings

FDC API (Food Data)

  • Purpose - Nutritional information for foods
  • Data shared - Food search queries (no personal information)
  • Privacy policy - https://fdc.nal.usda.gov/privacy-policy.html

OpenFoodFacts (Food Data)

  • Purpose - Barcode scanning and food database
  • Data shared - Barcode queries (no personal information)
  • Privacy policy - https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access Your Data

  • View all data associated with your account through the app
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

Delete Your Account

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Access the "Delete Account" option in Settings

Export Your Data

  • Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Contact us to request data export

Opt-Out of Analytics

  • Disable analytics tracking in app settings
  • Analytics are anonymized and do not include personal identifiers

Correct Your Data

  • Update your profile information, goals, and preferences at any time
  • Edit meal logs and nutrition data

Children's Privacy

NutriDuel is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption - All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Authentication - Secure password hashing and authentication protocols
  • Access controls - Row-level security ensures only you can access your data
  • Regular updates - We keep our systems and dependencies up to date with security patches

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • In-app notification - Alerting you when you open the app
  • Email notification - Sending an email to your registered address (for significant changes)
  • Updated date - Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of NutriDuel after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using NutriDuel, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.
